Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play Sonobender?

Point your device's camera at your hands. Move your right hand up and down to control pitch, and your left hand to control volume. The app uses machine learning to track your hand positions in real-time.

Why isn't my hand being tracked?

Make sure you have good lighting and your hands are clearly visible to the camera. The app works best with a plain background. Try holding your hands 1-2 feet away from the camera.

How do I change the sound or visual effects?

Tap the settings icon to access customization options. You can change the Aurora theme, adjust audio settings, and personalize the visual effects.

What are coins used for?

Coins can be used to unlock premium themes and remove ads temporarily. You can earn coins by watching rewarded videos or purchase them directly in the app.

How do I restore my purchases?

Go to Settings within the app and look for the "Restore Purchases" option. This will restore any previously purchased items linked to your Apple ID.

Why does the app need camera access?

Sonobender uses your camera exclusively for hand tracking to control the theremin. No video or images are recorded, stored, or transmitted. All processing happens locally on your device.

How do I disable personalized ads?

When you first open the app, you'll see a prompt asking for permission to track. Select "Ask App Not to Track" to disable personalized advertising. You can also change this later in your device's Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Tracking.

Troubleshooting

The app is crashing or freezing

  • Make sure you have the latest version from the App Store
  • Try closing and reopening the app
  • Restart your device
  • If the issue persists, please contact us with your device model and iOS version

No sound is playing

  • Check that your device isn't on silent mode
  • Make sure volume is turned up
  • Check if headphones are connected
  • Try restarting the app
