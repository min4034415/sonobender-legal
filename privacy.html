Privacy Policy for Sonobender

Last Updated: December 2025

Overview

Sonobender ("we", "our", or "the app") is a theremin music app that uses your device's camera to track hand movements. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

Camera Data

  • What: Hand position data from your device's camera
  • Why: To control the theremin sound based on hand movements
  • Storage: Processed locally on your device. We do not transmit camera footage or hand tracking data to any servers.

Device Identifiers (with your consent)

  • What: Advertising identifier (IDFA)
  • Why: To deliver personalized ads
  • Your Control: You can deny tracking permission when prompted, and personalized ads will be disabled

Analytics Data

  • What: App usage patterns, crash reports, performance data
  • Why: To improve app stability and features
  • Provider: Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics (Google)

Purchase Data

  • What: In-app purchases (processed by Apple)
  • Why: To unlock premium features
  • Storage: We do not store payment information. All transactions are handled by Apple.

Your Rights

For California Residents (CCPA)

  • Right to Know: Request what data we collect about you
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your data
  • Right to Opt-Out: We do not "sell" personal information as defined by CCPA. You can opt out of personalized advertising by denying App Tracking Transparency.

For European Users (GDPR)

  • Consent: We request your consent before enabling tracking
  • Access: You may request a copy of your data
  • Erasure: You may request deletion of your data
  • Portability: You may request your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

Data Retention

  • Analytics data: Retained for 14 months by Firebase
  • Crash data: Retained for 90 days
  • Purchase data: Retained as required by Apple

Children's Privacy

Sonobender is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app or on our website.
